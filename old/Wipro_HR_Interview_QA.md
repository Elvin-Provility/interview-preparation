# Top 10 Wipro HR Interview Questions + Sample Answers

## 1. Tell me about yourself

I am a Java full-stack developer with around 4 years of experience
working with Spring Boot, Angular/React, REST APIs, and
microservices-based applications. I have worked on building scalable
backend services, frontend UI development, and performance optimization.
I am now looking for opportunities to contribute to enterprise-level
projects and grow professionally.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 2. Why do you want to join Wipro?

Wipro is known for delivering large-scale digital transformation
projects and working with global clients. I am interested in gaining
exposure to enterprise environments and advanced technologies. The
company's learning culture aligns with my long-term career goals.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 3. Why are you looking for a job change?

I am grateful for my current experience, but I am now looking for new
challenges, exposure to larger projects, and opportunities to grow
technically and professionally.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 4. What are your strengths?

My strengths include strong problem-solving skills, quick learning
ability, and ownership of tasks. I focus on writing clean and
maintainable code and collaborate effectively with cross-functional
teams.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 5. What is your weakness?

Earlier, I used to spend extra time perfecting implementation details,
but I have learned to balance quality with deadlines by prioritizing
tasks better.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 6. How do you handle pressure or tight deadlines?

I break down tasks into smaller milestones, prioritize based on impact,
and communicate clearly with stakeholders to ensure timely delivery.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 7. Describe a challenging situation you faced.

In one project, we faced performance issues due to heavy frontend
rendering. I analyzed the bottleneck, optimized the rendering logic, and
improved response time significantly.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 8. Are you comfortable working in teams?

Yes, I enjoy collaborative environments and regularly work with backend,
frontend, QA, and DevOps teams. Clear communication is key to successful
delivery.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 9. Where do you see yourself in 5 years?

I see myself growing into a senior developer or technical lead role,
contributing to architecture decisions and mentoring junior developers.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 10. Do you have any questions for us?

-   What technologies are primarily used in this project?
-   What learning opportunities does Wipro provide for developers?
-   How is success measured for this role?
